CKA - Certified Kubernetes Administrator

What is CKA?

The **Certified Kubernetes Administrator (CKA)** certification is a highly regarded credential for IT professionals who work with Kubernetes. It is awarded by the Cloud Native Computing Foundation (CNCF) and proves your ability to deploy, manage, and troubleshoot Kubernetes clusters in production environments.

Why is CKA Important?

As Kubernetes has become the de facto standard for container orchestration, having a CKA certification demonstrates your knowledge and expertise in managing Kubernetes clusters. It helps organizations ensure that their Kubernetes environments are secure, reliable, and efficient. This certification is ideal for DevOps professionals, system administrators, and cloud engineers.

CKA Exam Topics

  • Cluster Architecture, Installation, and Configuration
  • Workloads and Scheduling
  • Services and Networking
  • Storage
  • Logging and Monitoring
  • Security
  • Troubleshooting
